Description

Ingridients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups plus 1 tablespoon (if needed) of maize, millet, or sorghum flour (can be mixed with cassava flour)

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a large pot, then pour in some of the maize flour
  2. Allow a minute for the sprinkled maize flour to absorb some heat then add flour in small portions and continue thorough mixing the ugali dough pressing the dough against the pot with the cooking stick. The thorough mixing ensures a uniform mixture which with constant heating and turning the flour, a nice piece of well-cooked ugali is obtained
  3. At this point the meal is ready to be served, you get to enjoy with the vegetable of choice like cassava leaves.
